1Introduction & Scope

PGpilots ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy and security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, share, and protect information when you use our platform.

This Policy applies to:

  • PG Owners — individuals who register and manage PG properties on our platform

By using PGpilots, you consent to the data practices described in this Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the Service.

ℹ️ This Policy is compliant with India's Information Technology Act, 2000 and the Information Technology (Reasonable Security Practices and Procedures and Sensitive Personal Data or Information) Rules, 2011.

3How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery — Providing, maintaining, and improving the PGpilots platform
  • Authentication — Verifying your identity via OTP and password login
  • Account Management — Managing your subscription, plan limits, and account settings
  • Communications — Sending service-related notifications, OTPs, and support responses

✅ We do NOT use your data for targeted advertising. We do NOT sell your data to third parties. We do NOT share your data with advertisers.

4Information Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers — We use Google Firebase (authentication, database, hosting) to operate the platform. Firebase processes data in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy and our Data Processing Agreement.
  • Legal Requirements — We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or government authority under Indian law.
  • Business Transfers — In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, user data may be transferred. We will notify you via the app or registered contact before such transfer.
  • Safety — If we believe disclosure is necessary to prevent harm to any person or to protect our rights.
  • With Your Consent — We may share data for other purposes with your explicit consent.
ℹ️ Firebase Data Processing: All data is stored on Google Firebase servers. Google acts as a data processor on our behalf and is contractually bound to protect your data. See Firebase Privacy.

5Data Storage & Security

We implement the following security measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption in Transit — All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
  • Encryption at Rest — Data stored in Firebase Firestore is encrypted at rest by Google
  • Authentication — Phone OTP + password authentication with Firebase Auth
  • Access Controls — Firestore security rules ensure each PG owner can only access their own data
  • Password Security — Passwords are never stored in plain text; they are managed securely by Firebase Authentication
  • Security Rules — Database-level rules prevent unauthorized cross-account data access
⚠️ While we implement strong security measures, no system is 100% secure. We encourage you to use a strong, unique password and keep your PG Code confidential. Report any suspected security breach to us immediately.

Data Location: Your data is stored on Google Firebase servers, which may be located in the United States, Europe, or Asia. All processing is subject to Google's enterprise-grade security standards.

6Tenant Data & PG Owner Responsibilities

PG owners enter tenant personal data (including sensitive data like ID numbers) into the platform. With respect to this data:

  • The PG owner is the data controller for tenant data. PGpilots acts as a data processor/service provider.
  • PG owners are responsible for obtaining proper consent from tenants before entering their data
  • PG owners must inform tenants that their data is being stored digitally in a cloud-based management system
  • PG owners are responsible for complying with any tenant requests to access or delete their data
  • Tenant ID data (Aadhaar, PAN numbers) must be collected only as permitted by applicable law
🔴 PGpilots does not share tenant data with any third party other than our infrastructure provider (Firebase/Google). Tenants' personal information is never used for marketing or advertising.

If a tenant contacts PGpilots directly regarding their data, we will refer them to the respective PG owner as the data controller, while assisting within our capacity as a processor.

10Children's Privacy
