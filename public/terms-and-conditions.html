2Description of Service

PGpilots is a cloud-based property management Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) platform designed specifically for Paying Guest (PG) accommodation owners and operators in India. The Service provides:

  • Tenant Management — Add, edit, and manage tenant profiles including personal details, ID proofs, and emergency contacts
  • Room Management — Track rooms, bed occupancy, and availability in real time
  • Rent Collection — Record, track, and manage rent payments with support for partial payments and late penalty configuration
  • Electricity Billing — Add monthly electricity bills and split them among room tenants
  • Reports & Analytics — Generate rent collection, occupancy, and tenant history reports with PDF download
  • Notifications — Alerts for overdue payments and upcoming due dates
